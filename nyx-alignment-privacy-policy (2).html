Sensitive information: We collect certain categories of sensitive personal information, including date of birth, exact birth time, gender identity, and romantic/sexual preference. This information is used solely to provide the core features of the App and is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

Birth data for astrological calculations: Your date of birth, exact birth time, and birth location are used to calculate your natal chart and derive astrological compatibility scores. Planetary placements (sun, moon, rising, venus, mars, mercury, jupiter, saturn, north node) are calculated on your device and then stored in your profile. This data is not shared with third parties in a personally identifiable form.

Location: We collect your precise GPS location continuously while the App is open (foreground only). We do not collect your location in the background when the App is closed. Location is used to calculate and display your approximate distance from other users. Your exact coordinates are not shown to other users.

Music preference: If you choose to add an on-repeat song to your profile, you enter this manually as free text. NYX Alignment is not connected to Spotify, Apple Music, or any other streaming service. No streaming account data is collected.

Voice messages: Voice recordings made in chat are stored on your device only and are not uploaded to our servers.

Verification information: If you choose to verify your profile, we may collect a phone number (hashed server-side), an email confirmation, and/or an identity selfie photo (stored in a private storage bucket). See Section 7 for full details.

4. Analytics and Tracking

The App contains no analytics, crash reporting, or behavioural tracking SDKs. No data is sent to services such as Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase Analytics, Segment, or similar platforms.

5. Push Notifications

With your permission, the App sends the following push notifications:

  • Single align: when another user aligns with you
  • Mutual alignment: when you and another user have both aligned — includes the other user's display name
  • Birthday: a local on-device notification on your birthday (non-subscribers only) — nothing is sent to any server
  • Cosmic Spotlight activated: when your Spotlight boost becomes active
  • Cosmic Spotlight 12-hour warning: 12 hours before your Spotlight expires
  • Cosmic Spotlight expired: when your Spotlight boost ends

Push tokens are stored in your profile in Supabase. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data is stored in Supabase's PostgreSQL database with row-level security enforced.
  • Profile photos are stored in Supabase Storage in a public bucket. Verification selfies are stored in a private bucket accessible only to authorised admin processes.
  • Session tokens are encrypted on your device using expo-secure-store.
  • Passwords are hashed by Supabase and never stored in plain text.
  • Phone numbers are hashed server-side; only the last 4 digits are retained.
  • Certain preferences and notification state are cached locally on your device in AsyncStorage.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access to your account.

7. Profile Verification

NYX Alignment offers optional three-step profile verification:

  • Phone verification: You enter your phone number in international format. A one-time passcode is sent via SMS. Your phone number is hashed server-side — the raw number is never stored.
  • Email verification: A confirmation link is sent to your registered email address.
  • Selfie verification (optional): You capture a live photo using your camera. This is uploaded to a private storage bucket and reviewed by an admin who compares it against your profile photos. On approval, a verified badge is displayed on your profile.

Important limitations: Verification badges indicate that a user has completed certain identity steps at the time of verification. They do not guarantee a user's ongoing identity, character, honesty, intentions, or safety. Verified profiles have not been subject to criminal background checks. You should not rely on verification status as a guarantee of another user's authenticity or suitability.

8. Safety Features and Moderation Data

NYX Alignment includes the following safety features:

  • Safety Centre: accessible from Settings, covering dating safety, reporting, blocking, verification, community guidelines, and emergency resources.
  • Report a profile or message: available from every profile and chat with nine report categories.
  • Block a user: available from every profile and chat. Blocking removes the user from your Discovery, Messages, and Matches.
  • Contact information warning: the App detects when phone numbers, email addresses, or external platform handles are typed in chat and shows a reminder that information shared outside NYX Alignment cannot be controlled by us.
  • First meet-up safety prompt: shown once per conversation after extended messaging, with guidance on meeting safely in person.
  • Safety reminder banners: shown periodically during Discovery with safety-focused prompts.

When you submit a safety report, we collect and retain the report content, reason, and any supporting details. Safety reports and moderation records may be retained after account deletion where necessary to protect other users, resolve disputes, or comply with applicable law. We do not disclose the identity of users who submit reports except where required by law.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view and update your profile information within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete your account from within the App. Deletion removes your profile, photos, messages, swipes, matches, blocks, and purchase records. This action is irreversible. Safety and moderation records may be retained as described in Section 8.
  • Push notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Location: You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. Distance-based features will not function without location access.

If you are located in Australia, you have rights under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. To make a privacy complaint or request access to your data, contact us at hello@nyxalignment.com.

10. Age Verification and Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

To help enforce this requirement and comply with Australian law (including the Online Safety Amendment (Social Media Minimum Age) Act 2024), we use Apple's Declared Age Range API to verify that users meet the minimum age requirement before creating an account. This verification is conducted at the device level by Apple and does not require government-issued identification. No additional personal information is collected as part of this process.

We also ask users to self-attest that they are 18 or older during onboarding, and we enforce a date-of-birth check that blocks account creation for users under 18.

If you believe a person under 18 has created an account, contact us at hello@nyxalignment.com and we will delete the account promptly.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted from our systems. We do not retain backup copies of deleted accounts beyond standard database backup cycles (typically 30 days).

Exceptions: Safety reports, moderation records, and information relevant to unresolved complaints or legal matters may be retained beyond account deletion where necessary to protect other users or comply with applicable law.
