4. Analytics and Tracking

The App contains no analytics, crash reporting, or behavioural tracking SDKs. No data is sent to services such as Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase Analytics, Segment, or similar platforms.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data is stored in Supabase's PostgreSQL database, hosted on Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Profile photos and verification selfies are stored in Supabase Storage (profile photos in a public bucket; selfies in a private bucket).
  • Session tokens are encrypted on your device using expo-secure-store.
  • Passwords are hashed by Supabase and never stored in plain text.
  • Phone numbers entered for contact blocking are hashed server-side; only the last 4 digits are retained.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view and update your profile information within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete your account from within the App. Deletion removes your profile, photos, messages, swipes, matches, and blocks from our systems. This action is irreversible.
  • Push notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Location: You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. Note that distance-based features will not function without location access.

If you are located in Australia, you have rights under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. If you wish to make a privacy complaint or request access to your data, please contact us at hello@nyxalignment.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted from our systems. We do not retain backup copies of deleted accounts beyond standard database backup cycles (typically 30 days).

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a person under 18 has created an account, please contact us at hello@nyxalignment.com and we will delete the account.
